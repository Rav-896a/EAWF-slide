The Roots of Sisterhood

Legacy of the East Asian Women's Forum (1994–2006)

1993 Manila: The vision was born at the Asia-Pacific Women's NGO Forum.

1994 Enoshima: The 1st EAWF set sail, building regional solidarity.

1996–2006: A decade of connection spanning Seoul, Ulaanbaatar, Taipei, Hong Kong, and Beijing.

The Essence: Establishing a "Sisterhood" mechanism to address shared structural challenges in East Asia.

Rebirth & Strategic Shift

Facing Contemporary Challenges (2022–Present)

  • Why Restart? Navigating new risks: Digital Transformation, Geopolitics, and Super-Aging Societies.
  • Transformation: Moving from "experience sharing" to "action-oriented co-creation."
  • Focus Areas: GBV prevention, political participation, care economy, and regional security.
